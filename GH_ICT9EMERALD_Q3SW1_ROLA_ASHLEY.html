PAW PATROL

SYNOPSIS
The series focuses on a young boy named Ryder who leads a crew of search and rescue dogs that call themselves the PAW Patrol. They work together on missions to protect the shoreside community of Adventure Bay and surrounding areas.
CHARACTERS
MARSHALL SKYE CHASE RYDER ZUMA ROCKY
In the PAW Patrol, Marshall is usually the comic relief of the group, as he tends to be goofy and clumsy. Skye is a fearless and smart Cockapoo who loves to take off in her helicopter or activate the wings in her pup pack. CHASE is a German Shepard puppy and main character of the PAW Patrol. He is a police and traffic cop dog and a spy dog as of Season 2. Ryder is the leader, rescuer, and teacher of the pups. He has heart, a can-do-spirit, and the skills PAW Patrol needs to get the job done! Zuma is one of the seven main protagonists in the TV series, PAW Patrol. He is a male Chocolate Labrador pup and the water rescuer of the PAW Patrol. Rocky is one of the seven main protagonists in the PAW Patrol series. He is a male mixed breed Eco pup and the fifth member of the PAW Patrol.
